Museum of Dessert

we debut our famous birthday cake (after two years in the test kitchen) AND our classic b’day truffles. fun fact: cake truffles started as a snack we made for ourselves from cake scraps, but they were so darn good that we added them to our official menu. once we got birthday cake crumbs down, we moved on to our larger quest of making a funfetti cake, canned frosting and all, from scratch. turns out that looking on the side of the cake mix box at the master ingredient list was really helpful in getting the “secret” stuff we couldn’t figure out by taste.
